📖 Acronyms
TOI TESS Object of Interest — a star flagged by the TESS mission as hosting a potential transiting planet
TIC TESS Input Catalog — the reference catalogue of stars observed and characterised by the TESS mission
MCS Mission Candidate Sample — the full list of candidate targets for the Ariel mission
DR3 Gaia Data Release 3 — provides precise astrometry and the unique source_id identifier for cross-matching stellar catalogues
TPC TESS Planetary Candidate — unconfirmed planet candidates from the TESS pipeline included in the MCS
